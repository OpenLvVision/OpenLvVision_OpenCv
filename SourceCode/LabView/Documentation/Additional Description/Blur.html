The function smooths an image using the kernel:

{{< katex >}}$ \texttt{K} = \frac{1}{\texttt{ksize.width} \cdot \texttt{ksize.height}} \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 & 1 \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix}${{< /katex >}}

The call blur(src, dst, ksize, anchor, borderType) is equivalent to boxFilter(src, dst, src.type(), ksize, anchor, true, borderType).
