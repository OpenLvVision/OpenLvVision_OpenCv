The function calculates a 2 × 3 affine transformation matrix such that:

{{< katex >}}$ \begin{bmatrix} x'_i \\ y'_i \end{bmatrix} = \texttt{map\_matrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} x_i \\ y_i \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} ${{< /katex >}}

where

{{< katex >}}$ \texttt{dst}(i) = (x'_i, y'_i), \quad \texttt{src}(i) = (x_i, y_i), \quad i = 0,1,2 ${{< /katex >}}
